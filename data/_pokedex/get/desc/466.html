Heedless of enemy attacks, it closes in, shoves its tails onto the foe, then looses high voltage. The instant it presses the tips of its tails onto an opponent, it sends over 20,000 volts of electricity into the foe. As its electric charge amplifies, blue sparks begin to crackle between its horns. It pushes the tips of its two tails against the foe, then lets loose with over 20,000 volts of power. When it gets excited, it thumps its chest. With every thud, thunder roars and electric sparks shower all around. It grips its tail, which spews electricity, and then beats down opponents with the power of its electrified fist.
