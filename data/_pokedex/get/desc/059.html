Its proud and regal appearance has captured the hearts of people since long ago. The sight of it running over 6,200 miles in a single day and night has captivated many people. A Pokémon that has long been admired for its beauty. It runs agilely as if on wings. Its magnificent bark conveys a sense of majesty. Anyone hearing it can’t help but grovel before it. Arcanine is known for its high speed. It is said to be capable of running over 6,200 miles in a single day and night. The fire that blazes wildly within this Pokémon’s body is its source of power. The fire burning inside its body serves as the energy to fuel it as it runs great distances. It appears in many legends. Legends tell of its fighting alongside a general and conquering a whole country.
