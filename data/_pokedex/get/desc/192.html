It gets energy from warm sunlight and is known for its habit of moving in pursuit of it. Since it converts sunlight into energy, it is always looking in the direction of the sun. As the hot season approaches, the petals on this Pokémon’s face become more vivid and lively. Sunflora converts solar energy into nutrition. It moves around actively in the daytime when it is warm. It stops moving as soon as the sun goes down for the night.
