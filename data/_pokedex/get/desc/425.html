Because of the way it floats aimlessly, an old folktale calls it a “Signpost for Wandering Spirits.” These Pokémon are called the “Signpost for Wandering Spirits.” Children holding them sometimes vanish. A Pokémon formed by the spirits of people and Pokémon. It loves damp, humid seasons. If for some reason its body bursts, its soul spills out with a screaming sound. Its round body is stuffed with souls and expands each time it leads someone away.
