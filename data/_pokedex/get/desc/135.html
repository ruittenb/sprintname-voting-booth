If agitated, it uses electricity to straighten out its fur and launch it in small bunches. By storing electricity in its body, it can shoot its bristlelike fur like a barrage of missiles. Every hair on its body starts to stand sharply on end if it becomes charged with electricity. It accumulates negative ions in the atmosphere to blast out 10,000-volt lightning bolts. Jolteon’s cells generate a low level of electricity. This power is amplified by the static electricity of its fur, enabling the Pokémon to drop thunderbolts. The bristling fur is made of electrically charged needles. When its fur stands on end, that’s a sign it’s about to give off a jolt of electricity. Take care, as sometimes lightning strikes next to it, too. Its lungs contain an organ that creates electricity. The crackling sound of electricity can be heard when it exhales.
