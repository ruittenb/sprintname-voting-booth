One of the legendary bird Pokémon. It is said that its appearance indicates the coming of spring. It is said to be the legendary bird Pokémon of fire. Every flap of its wings creates a dazzling flare of flames. Moltres is a legendary bird Pokémon that has the ability to control fire. If this Pokémon is injured, it is said to dip its body in the molten magma of a volcano to burn and heal itself.
