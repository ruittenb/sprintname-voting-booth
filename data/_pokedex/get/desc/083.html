It can’t live without the stalk it holds. That’s why it defends the stalk from attackers with its life. The plant stalk it holds is its weapon. The stalk is used like a sword to cut all sorts of things. It always walks about with a plant stalk clamped in its beak. The stalk is used for building its nest. Farfetch’d is always seen with a stalk from a plant of some sort. Apparently, there are good stalks and bad stalks. This Pokémon has been known to fight with others over stalks.
