It makes its nest in the shade of corals. If it senses danger, it spits murky ink and flees. Known to shoot down flying bugs with precision blasts of ink from the surface of the water. Horsea eats small insects and moss off of rocks. If the ocean current turns fast, this Pokémon anchors itself by wrapping its tail around rocks or coral to prevent being washed away. If Horsea senses danger, it will reflexively spray a dense black ink from its mouth and try to escape. This Pokémon swims by cleverly flapping the fin on its back.
