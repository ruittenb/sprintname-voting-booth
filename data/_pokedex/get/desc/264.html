It charges prey at speeds over 60 mph. However, because it can only run straight, it often fails. When running in a straight line, it can easily top 60 miles an hour. It has a tough time with curved roads. Linoone always runs full speed and only in straight lines. If facing an obstacle, it makes a right-angle turn to evade it. This Pokémon is very challenged by gently curving roads. When hunting, Linoone will make a beeline straight for the prey at a full run. While this Pokémon is capable of topping 60 mph, it has to come to a screeching halt before it can turn.
