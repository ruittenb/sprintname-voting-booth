It has the stamina to fly all day on its broad wings. It fights by using its sharp beak. With its huge and magnificent wings, it can keep aloft without ever having to land for rest. Fearow is recognized by its long neck and elongated beak. They are conveniently shaped for catching prey in soil or water. It deftly moves its long and skinny beak to pluck prey. Drawings of a Pokémon resembling Fearow can be seen in murals from deep in ancient history. Carrying food through Fearow’s territory is dangerous. It will snatch the food away from you in a flash!
