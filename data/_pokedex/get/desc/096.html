It can tell what people are dreaming by sniffing with its big nose. It loves fun dreams. Puts enemies to sleep, then eats their dreams. Occasionally gets sick from eating only bad dreams. If you think that you had a good dream but you can’t remember it, a Drowzee has probably eaten it. If your nose becomes itchy while you are sleeping, it’s a sure sign that one of these Pokémon is standing above your pillow and trying to eat your dream through your nostrils. It finds really fun dreams tasty. When it makes friends with people, it may show them the most delicious dreams it’s ever eaten. It can be spotted near recreational facilities, intending to eat the pleasant dreams of children who enjoyed themselves there that day.
