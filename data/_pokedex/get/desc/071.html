It pools in its mouth a fluid with a honey-like scent, which is really an acid that dissolves anything. Said to live in huge colonies deep in jungles, although no one has ever returned from there. Once ingested into this Pokémon’s body, even the hardest object will melt into nothing. Victreebel has a long vine that extends from its head. This vine is waved and flicked about as if it were an animal to attract prey. When an unsuspecting prey draws near, this Pokémon swallows it whole.
