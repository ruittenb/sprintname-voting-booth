The fate of the ships and crew that wander into Jellicent’s habitat: all sunken, all lost, all vanished. They propel themselves by expelling absorbed seawater from their bodies. Their favorite food is life energy. Its body is mostly seawater. It’s said there’s a castle of ships Jellicent have sunk on the seafloor. Jellicent is always hanging around fancy cruise ships and tankers, hoping to drag away its prey.
