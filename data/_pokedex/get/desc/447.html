It has the peculiar power of being able to see emotions such as joy and rage in the form of waves. It uses the shapes of auras, which change according to emotion, to communicate with others. The aura that emanates from its body intensifies to alert others if it is afraid or sad. It’s tough enough to run right through the night, and it’s also a hard worker, but it’s still just a youngster. It knows how people and Pokémon feel by looking at their auras. It doesn’t approach dangerous opponents.
