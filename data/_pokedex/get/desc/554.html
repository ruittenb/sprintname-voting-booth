When its internal fire is burning, it cannot calm down and it runs around. When the fire diminishes, it falls asleep. Darumaka’s droppings are hot, so people used to put them in their clothes to keep themselves warm. When it sleeps, it pulls its limbs into its body and its internal fire goes down to 1,100° F.
