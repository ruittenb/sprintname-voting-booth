A Pokémon that appears to be a plant. It captures unwary prey by dousing them with a toxic powder. The leafy parts act as cutters for slashing foes. It spits a fluid that dissolves everything. It spits out Poison Powder to immobilize the enemy and then finishes it with a spray of Acid. Weepinbell has a large hook on its rear end. At night, the Pokémon hooks on to a tree branch and goes to sleep. If it moves around in its sleep, it may wake up to find itself on the ground.
