It has black, cape-like fur that is very hard and decreases the amount of damage it receives. It loyally follows its Trainer’s orders. For ages, they have helped Trainers raise Pokémon. This very loyal Pokémon helps Trainers, and it also takes care of other Pokémon. This Pokémon obeys its master’s orders faithfully.
