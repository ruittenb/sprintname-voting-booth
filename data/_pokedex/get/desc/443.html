It attacks using its huge mouth. While its attacks are powerful, it hurts itself out of clumsiness, too. It nests in horizontal holes warmed by geothermal heat. Foes who get too close can expect to be pounced on and bitten. It nests in small, horizontal holes in cave walls. It pounces to catch prey that stray too close. It skulks in caves, and when prey or an enemy passes by, it leaps out and chomps them. The force of its attack sometimes chips its teeth. It reacts to anything that moves—flies right at it and bites it. Sometimes it injures itself, but it doesn’t care too much.
