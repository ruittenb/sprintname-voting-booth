The special membrane enveloping Alomomola has the ability to heal wounds. Floating in the open sea is how they live. When they find a wounded Pokémon, they embrace it and bring it to shore. It gently holds injured and weak Pokémon in its fins. Its special membrane heals their wounds. They float upon the open sea. Many water Pokémon gather in the area around Alomomola. Fishermen take them along on long voyages, because if you have an Alomomola with you, there’ll be no need for a doctor or medicine.
