It lives in caves on the seafloor and creates giant whirlpools every time it moves. It is said that it usually hides in underwater caves. It can create whirlpools by yawning. It stores energy by sleeping at underwater depths at which no other life-forms can survive. Kingdra lives at extreme ocean depths that are otherwise uninhabited. It has long been believed that the yawning of this Pokémon creates spiraling ocean currents. Kingdra sleeps on the seafloor where it is otherwise devoid of life. When a storm arrives, the Pokémon is said to awaken and wander about in search of prey.
