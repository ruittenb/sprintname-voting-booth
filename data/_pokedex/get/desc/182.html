When the heavy rainfall season ends, it is drawn out by warm sunlight to dance in the open. Bellossom gather at times and seem to dance. They say that the dance is a ritual to summon the sun. When Bellossom gets exposed to plenty of sunlight, the leaves ringing its body begin to spin around. This Pokémon’s dancing is renowned in the southern lands. A Bellossom grows flowers more beautifully if it has evolved from a smelly Gloom—the more stinky the better. At night, this Pokémon closes its petals and goes to sleep.
