It whittles its constantly growing fangs by gnawing on hard things. It can chew apart cinder walls. With its long fangs, this surprisingly violent Pokémon can gnaw away even thick concrete with ease. The webs on its hind legs enable it to cross rivers. It searches wide areas for food. Raticate’s sturdy fangs grow steadily. To keep them ground down, it gnaws on rocks and logs.
