The tip of its forked tail quivers when it is predicting its opponent’s next move. Espeon is extremely loyal to any Trainer it considers to be worthy. It is said that this Pokémon developed its precognitive powers to protect its Trainer from harm. It unleashes psychic power from the orb on its forehead. When its power is exhausted, the orb grows dull and dark. Although it originally had no powers at all, people say its precognitive faculties were awakened by its need to protect itself.
