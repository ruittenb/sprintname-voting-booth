To share its happiness, it flies around the world seeking kind-hearted people. It grows dispirited if it is not with kind people. It can float in midair without moving its wings. They say that it will appear before kindhearted, caring people and shower them with happiness. Togetic is said to be a Pokémon that brings good fortune. When the Pokémon spots someone who is pure of heart, it is said to appear and share its happiness with that person.
