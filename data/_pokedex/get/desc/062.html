With its extremely tough muscles, it can keep swimming in the Pacific Ocean without resting. A swimmer adept at both the front crawl and breaststroke. Easily overtakes the best human swimmers. Poliwrath’s highly developed, brawny muscles never grow fatigued, however much it exercises. It is so tirelessly strong, this Pokémon can swim back and forth across the ocean without effort. Its percentage of body fat is nearly zero. Its body is entirely muscle, which makes it heavy and forces its swimming prowess to develop. It’s quite a gifted swimmer, even among Water-type Pokémon, but it normally spends its time on land.
