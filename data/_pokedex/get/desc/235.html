It marks its territory by using its tail like a paintbrush. There are more than 5,000 different marks. Once it becomes an adult, it has a tendency to let its comrades plant footprints on its back. Smeargle marks the boundaries of its territory using a body fluid that leaks out from the tip of its tail. Over 5,000 different marks left by this Pokémon have been found. It draws symbols all over the place to mark its territory. In towns with many Smeargle, the walls are covered in graffiti. The fluid of Smeargle’s tail secretions changes in the intensity of its hue as the Pokémon’s emotions change.
