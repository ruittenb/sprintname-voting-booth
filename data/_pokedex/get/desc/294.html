The shock waves from its cries can tip over trucks. It stamps its feet to power up. It shouts loudly by inhaling air, and then uses its well-developed stomach muscles to exhale. Loudred’s bellowing can completely decimate a wood-frame house. It uses its voice to punish its foes. This Pokémon’s round ears serve as loudspeakers. Loudred shouts while stamping its feet. After it finishes shouting, this Pokémon becomes incapable of hearing anything for a while. This is considered to be a weak point.
