It likes to carry around a small rock. It may wander around others’ feet and cause them to stumble. It carries a round, egg-shaped rock in its belly pouch and gives the rock to its friends. It carefully carries a round, white rock that it thinks is an egg. It’s bothered by how curly its hair looks. It’s too small to lay eggs yet. As a surrogate, it searches out round white stones. Usually it’s a well-behaved Pokémon, but if you take its round rock away, it will cry and fuss and throw a big old tantrum.
