It often plants its root feet in the ground during the day and sows seeds as it walks about at night. During the day, it stays in the cold underground to avoid the sun. It grows by bathing in moonlight. Its scientific name is “Oddium Wanderus.” At night, it is said to walk nearly 1,000 feet on its two roots. During the daytime, Oddish buries itself in soil to absorb nutrients from the ground using its entire body. The more fertile the soil, the glossier its leaves become. Oddish searches for fertile, nutrient-rich soil, then plants itself. During the daytime, while it is planted, this Pokémon’s feet are thought to change shape and become similar to the roots of trees.
