It is said that when one runs at high speed, its wings create blades of wind that can fell nearby trees. When it folds up its body and extends its wings, it looks like a jet plane. It flies at sonic speed. It flies at speeds equal to a jet fighter plane. It never allows its prey to escape. The protuberances on its head serve as sensors. It can even detect distant prey. It flies at the speed of sound while searching for prey, and it has midair battles with Salamence as the two compete for food.
